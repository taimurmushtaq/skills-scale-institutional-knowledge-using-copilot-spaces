# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads ensure product quality by defining test strategies, coordinating testing activities, and validating that acceptance criteria and quality standards are met before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define test plans and quality acceptance criteria
- Coordinate automated and manual testing efforts
- Identify quality gaps and blockers
- Work with developers on test coverage and reproducibility
- Validate releases against acceptance criteria and success metrics
- Document known issues and testing status for release notes

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, high-quality releases with minimal defects
- Identify risks early in the development cycle
- Maintain user trust through consistent quality standards

### Typical Communication
- Quality status in daily standups and weekly syncs
- Test plan reviews with developers and product managers
- Release readiness sign-off in pre-deployment checklists

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Developers** on test coverage and reproducibility
- Coordinates with **Product Managers** on acceptance criteria validation
- Reports quality status to **Project Managers** for release planning
- Partners with **UX/Design Lead** on usability testing

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads guide architectural decisions, manage technical risk, and ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with long-term technical strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns
- Identify and assess technical risks and dependencies
- Review designs and guide implementation approach
- Mentor and level up the development team
- Ensure solutions meet performance, security, and scalability requirements
- Participate in code review for complex components

### Goals
- Build systems that are reliable, secure, and maintainable
- Reduce technical debt and future rework
- Enable the team to make informed technical trade-offs

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Guidance during planning on feasibility and effort
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Provides technical guidance to **Developers** during implementation
- Advises **Project Managers** on technical risks and timeline impacts
- Collaborates with **Stakeholders/Sponsors** on technical trade-offs and feasibility
- Partners with **QA/Testing Lead** on testability and performance requirements

---

## Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors provide business context, resource approval, and accountability for project outcomes. They represent customer/business needs and have authority to make go/no-go decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success criteria
- Allocate resources and provide budget oversight
- Make priority and trade-off decisions
- Remove blockers and escalate constraints
- Validate that delivered value meets business needs
- Communicate project status and decisions to leadership

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers measurable business value
- Maintain alignment between execution and business priorities
- Enable team to focus on delivery without organizational ambiguity

### Typical Communication
- Monthly or milestone-based status updates
- Go/no-go decision gates at initiation and release
- Escalation for resource conflicts or strategic changes

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Works with **Project Managers** on resource allocation and priority decisions
- Receives progress updates and risk escalations from **Product Managers**
- Approves go/no-go decisions at initiation and release gates
- Validates business value delivery with **Product Managers**

---

## UX/Design Lead

### Role Summary
UX/Design Leads ensure that solutions are user-centered, accessible, and aligned with product vision and brand standards. They guide design decisions and validate usability.

### Responsibilities
- Define user experience and interaction patterns
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Conduct or coordinate user research and usability testing
- Ensure accessibility and design consistency
- Collaborate with developers on implementation fidelity
- Validate delivered features meet design intent and user needs

### Goals
- Build products that are intuitive, delightful, and accessible
- Reduce user confusion and support burden
- Maintain product brand and design consistency

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Acceptance criteria definition with product and development
- Usability feedback and design adjustments during development

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Defines acceptance criteria with **Product Managers** and **Developers**
- Conducts usability testing with support from **QA/Testing Lead**
- Collaborates with **Developers** on implementation fidelity
- Ensures technical feasibility with **Technical Lead/Architect**
